Who We Are

We are The Paper Pigeon, a group of highschool students who like to work on history projects together! What you see in front of you is a handpicked list of our projects. From the Egyptian coast, to the French in Algeria, to the Suffragettes and the continued struggle for women's rights, The Paper Pigeon flies untethered!

Le Pigeon de Papier

Le Pigeon de Papier is a newspaper that chronicles many of the adventures The Paper Pigeon has flown on. Check out some of our available editions!

The Suffragettes

The Suffragettes were a prominent force advocating for women's rights in the 20th century. They believed that it takes more than passive protests to create lasting change, which is captured in their motto, Deeds, not words.
